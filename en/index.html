OUR STORY

Born from real pain. Built from Sapzurro.

Host Help wasn't born in a Silicon Valley lab. It was born in a wooden cabin in Sapzurro, Choco -- one of the most remote places in Colombia. No stable signal, no reliable local staff, no way to be there in person.

I'm Alejandro. I live in Medellin and own a property deep in the Darien jungle. Every day was the same: answering WhatsApp between meetings, coordinating cleaning by phone, chasing payments, checking if tasks got done, trying to create social media content, filing tax reports... all remote, all manual.

I couldn't hire a team of 10 people. But I needed one. So I built it.

Host Help is the team I wish I'd had from day one. Every feature you see exists because I needed it. Every automation solves a problem I lived through. This isn't theory -- it's real operations, battle-tested in the toughest conditions.

If it works for me -- managing a cabin in the Darien jungle from Medellin, with no team, no stable signal -- it works for any property owner. I'm sure you're going through the same thing.

-- Alejandro Dominguez, Founder of Host Help and owner of Cabana Sancibrian
