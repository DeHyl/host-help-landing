The 7 modules

The complete team
for your property

Not just a chatbot. A full AI team working at every stage of your property cycle — including tax compliance.

24/7 Guest Agent

Handles inquiries in natural language at any hour. Rates, directions, house rules, nearby activities — all without waking you at midnight.

Smart Maintenance

Guests report issues directly to the agent. Work orders are created automatically and tracked until resolved. No emergency calls to you.

Pre & Post Stay

Arrival instructions, access codes and a welcome message sent automatically before each stay. After checkout: a farewell note and review invite. Right on time, every time.

Mini Marketing Agency

A full marketing agency inside your dashboard. Creates the videos, publishes them on TikTok and Instagram, responds to comments and DMs, manages your ad budget on Meta and Google, analyzes what works, and proposes strategies to fill your booking calendar. What chain hotels achieve with a 5-person team — you get with one agent.

Property Showcase

Host Help automatically generates a dedicated website for your property with photos, description, rates and a booking button. Less dependence on Airbnb or Booking.

Identity & Regulatory Compliance

Your agent has its own name and personality — the voice of your property, not a generic bot. The system also monitors tourism registry renewals and alerts you before they expire.

Electronic Invoicing & Tax Compliance

Integrated with Alegra to automatically issue electronic invoices on every confirmed booking. Guest receives their invoice, you receive tax compliance — no extra effort.
